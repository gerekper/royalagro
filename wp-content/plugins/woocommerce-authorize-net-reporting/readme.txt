=== WooCommerce Authorize.Net Reporting ===
Author: skyverge
Tags: woocommerce
Requires at least: 5.2
Tested up to: 5.5.3
Requires PHP: 7.0

Automatically generates and emails daily CSV exports of your Authorize.Net transactions. You can also download an export by going to WooCommerce > Reports > Authorize.Net > Export

See https://docs.woocommerce.com/document/woocommerce-authorize-net-reporting/ for full documentation.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire 'woocommerce-authorize-net-reporting' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
